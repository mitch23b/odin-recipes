Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish of seasoned fillings in a folded or rolled tortilla that are eaten by hand

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Tortilla

Steps

  1. Brown meat
  2. Dice lettuce and tomatoes
  3. Shred cheese
  4. Fill tortillas
  5. Enjoy tacos
