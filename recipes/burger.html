Burger

Description

A burger is a sandwich made of a cooked ground meat patty on a bun or roll.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Bun
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Form meat into patties
  2. Grill patties for 5 minutes
  3. Place patties on bun
  4. Add toppings
  5. Enjoy your burger
